PMS
PMS / VED
Centre Management Hub
  • NPA applications with a "shipped" status are added to the shipment module of Apna Dashboard and their progress is tracked alongside the application status.
  • Pod management lifecycle data — including hours students used computers, hours of active learning, topics covered, offline and online activity, student attendance, and hours computers were powered on — is fetched from Apniprerna and shown beside the submitted weekly report for admin review.
  • Computers that have not been powered on for more than 3 days are automatically surfaced in the PMS complaint module, with data pulled from Apniprerna.
  • A callback request is created automatically for any such complaint to prompt follow-up and capture the reason.
  • POD leaders can create students in Apniprerna directly from the students stored in PMS.
  • Courses are accessible from the curriculum library of LMS.
  • Students added in PMS can be selected when submitting weekly reports, making it easy to track which student has been learning and for how long.
Connected to
AP
Apniprerna
Activity and Device Monitoring
  • Full PC control and OS-level access is made possible through Shiksha OS.
  • Complete device health data is available through Shiksha OS.
  • Student activity is fetched and categorised, then displayed in the weekly report activity section.
  • ALIS and student website access can be launched directly from the individual student page within PMS.
  • POD leaders can submit weekly reports directly from the Apniprerna dashboard and select which students were active.
  • Offline tracking data is used across reports, student activity summaries, and the student website.
  • When a student logs out of Shiksha OS, parents are automatically notified.

  • KPIs: POD and computer performance data is fetched directly from PMS.

What This Ecosystem Enables

Every product in this ecosystem is designed with one goal: to help each student grow with confidence, clarity, and the right support around them.

Personalized Learning Journey

Each student gets a learning experience shaped around their pace, gaps, and goals — powered by AI and adaptive curriculum tools.

Safe and Distraction-Free Environment

A purpose-built OS, controlled devices, and activity monitoring create a secure space where students can focus fully on learning.

Track Progress and Improve Continuously

Weekly reports, assessments, and activity data give students and educators a clear picture of where each learner stands and what to work on next.

Guidance, Support, and Community

Community Learning Centres, POD leaders, and parents stay connected and informed through shared data and timely notifications.

Build Skills and Real Knowledge

From AI tutoring on JEE and NEET to customized curricula, students develop practical knowledge they can apply — not just exam scores.

Better Opportunities for a Brighter Future

Digital literacy, structured learning, and ongoing support open doors to opportunities that were previously out of reach for many students.

A connected suite of software products built to support digital literacy, self-directed learning, and student growth in communities that need it most.
